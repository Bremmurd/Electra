About Us

ELECTRA | The Future To Hassle Free Voting

As much as the developers at Core Tech try to realise the dream of online voting, we may encounter some unnoticed errors. This is still a project in development and we keep improving the functionalities of ELECTRA that's why we are open to any discussion regarding the performance of the system.

By welcoming suggestions from users gives a wide scope of thinking. We also stand corrected as the system was designed to function as a basic electoral system handler. We tried as much to limit the number of errors that occur within the system but incase of any you can always alert us on the same.
